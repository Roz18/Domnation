Witch and her familiar

Domination for you and your imp

The bond between witches and their familiars is a sinister one, steeped in darkness and treachery. These so-called companions, also known as imps, take on the guise of harmless household pets, but their true purpose is far more ominous. As the legends tell it, familiars are nothing less than demonic guides, sent to aid witches in their malevolent spells. With unwavering loyalty, they stalk the earth in animal form, watching and waiting for their next victim. Beware the witch and her familiar, for they are a fearsome duo, united in their quest for dark magic and domination.
